



Reference for theme-specific Messages being broadcast throughout the theme and their origins:


ExitScreen
	Sent From:
		ScreenWithMenuElements Header

	Received by:
		Any screen that loads "_mouse.lua"

	Parameters:
		screen : name of the screen where the broadcast was made.


MouseLeftClick

	Sent From:
		Any screen that loads the _mouse.lua Actor

	Received by:
		All quadButton() instances.


MouseRightClick

	Sent From:
		Any screen that loads the _mouse.lua Actor


ProfileLeftClick
	
	Sent From:
		ScreenSelectProfile overlay

	Parameters:
		pn : PlayerNumber corresponding to the player whose side the profiles are being clicked on.
		index : integer index for the profiles listed on the scroller.
